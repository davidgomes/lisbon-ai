FAQ

What level of experience do I need?

Lisbon.ai is for engineers with experience in programming (think Software Engineers, Physics Engineers, Electrical Engineers, Biomedica Engineers or students who already have considerable experience) that want to get started in AI. Little to none exposure to AI is expected from the participants. More advanced engineers are welcome as well given some of the workshops are more advanced than others.

What do I need to take?

All that you need is your laptop (and charger!) for the workshop. Excitement and curiosity are most welcome as well!

How much are the tickets?

The early bird tickets are 17€ and the regular tickets are 32.5€ each.

Who is organizing this event?

You can see us in the Team page.

How can I up to date with you guys?

You can follow us on Twitter, subscribe to our newsletter or like us on Facebook.

Will there be food during the event?

Yes. The Lisbon.ai organizing team will take care of you. Providing food and drinks during the coffee breaks, lunch and the Drinks & Drinks moment.

What’s the Drinks & Drinks?

It’s the informal moment dedicated to networking after all the talks and workshops. It’s the moment you’ll be able to meet in person with speakers, sponsors and Lisbon.ai’s team.

Do you have a Code of Conduct?

Yes, we have a code of conduct that every participant must agree to in order to make our event safe and inclusive for everyone. You can read it here.
